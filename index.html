WELCOME TO YOUR PERSONAL TRAINNG HUB!


1. Workout Tips (Basics)

Warm-up is Key:

Workout shuru karne se pehle 5-10 mins warm-up zaroori hai injury se bachne ke liye. Shuruat mein zyada wazan uthane se zyada zaroori hai ki aapki exercise karne ki technique (form) sahi ho.

Rest Days:

Body ko recover hone ke liye hafte mein 1-2 din rest zaroori hai.

2. Diet & Nutrition (Sabse Important)

Protein Power:

Muscle building ke liye protein rich food jaise paneer, eggs, soya, ya chicken apni diet mein shamil karein.

Hydration:

Din bhar mein kam se kam 3-4 litre paani piyein, khaas karke workout ke dauran.

Pre-Workout Meal:

Workout se 45 min pehle ek kela (banana) ya black coffee lene se energy milti hai.

3. Knowledge Base (Chote-chote Facts)

BMI (Body Mass Index):

Kya aapko apna sahi BMI pata hai? Isse aapko pata chalta hai ki aap underweight hain ya overweight.

Calories:

Weight lose karne ke liye Calorie Deficit aur gain karne ke liye Calorie Surplus zaroori hai.

Muscle Recovery:

Workout ke baad protein shake lene se muscle recovery mein madad milti hai.
